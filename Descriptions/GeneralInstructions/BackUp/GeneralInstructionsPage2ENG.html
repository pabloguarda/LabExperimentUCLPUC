The animation of a given trip shown in the transportation network has seven parts:


1. Passenger walking from its origin location to a bus stop (Figur 1)

2. Waiting at the bus stop (represented by a number shown in the icon of the stop), Figure 2).

3. Bus arrival to the bus stop (Figure 3)

4. Passenger boarding to the bus (Figure 4)

5. Passenger travelling in the bus (Figure 5)

6. Bus arrival to the next bus stop (Figure 6)

7. Passenger walking to its destination (Figure 7)
