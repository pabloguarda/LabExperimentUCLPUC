• Welcome to the experiment. During the experiment, you will be experiencing different trips in public transport (of the type you will see in the next page). The virtual transportation network shown in the experiment was designed to mimic what you might experience while traveling through _city_’s public transport. The trips will all be on a bus and you will be travelling from one location to another. 
  • Sometimes you will be waiting at a bus stop or travelling on a bus, just like in real life. During the trips, you will wait on average between 4 and 5 seconds and never more than one minute. In this context, please imagine that 1 second in the experiment corresponds to 1 minute of waiting or travelling in real life.
